LIONESS




SYMPTOMS OF PERSISTENT DEPRESSIVE DISORDER WITH SUICIDAL TENDENCIES

.


If you realize that you have suffered any of these listed below continuously for long periods of times, at the same time and/or off and on for a while you should consult a professional for an evaluation.


  • Feeling sad or having a depressed mood.

  • Loss of interest in activities.

  • Changes in appetite

  • Weight loss or gain unrelated to dieting.

  • Trouble sleeping or sleeping too much.

  • Loss of energy or increased fatigue.

  • Increase in purposeless physical activity.

  • Pacing or slowed movements and speech

  • Feeling worthless or guilty.

  • Difficulty thinking or concentrating.

  • Less sociable

  • Irritable

  • Increasingly anxious

  • Anxiety
